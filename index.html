Meet Me

Dr. Nelson G. Agoyaoy

With two decades of heart-led service in the academe, Dr. Nelson Agoyaoy joins the school year 2024-2025 as the K-10 Coordinator of Student Concerns. Known to many as a "Model Teacher of the Year," Dr. Nelson believes that education extends far beyond the textbook.

As a Metrobank Foundation Outstanding Filipino awardee, Dr. Nelson brings a wealth of expertise to our Social Studies and Values Education departments. He enjoys life with his wife, Cherry May, a visual artist.
