The Educator

About Nelson G. Agoyaoy, Ed.D.

With over twenty years of experience in academia, Dr. Nelson G. Agoyaoy has built a distinguished legacy as an educator, administrator, and advocate for student development.

Currently, he serves as the K-10 Coordinator of Student Concerns (S.Y. 2024-2025) and is a dedicated faculty member at the University of the East Caloocan’s Basic Education Department, where he teaches Social Studies and Values Education.

Consultancy & Media

Beyond the classroom, Dr. Agoyaoy is a sought-after speaker and consultant for the Department of Education and the Philippine Public School Teachers Association. He is a frequent guest on major media platforms, including CNN Philippines, ABS-CBN News Channel, PTV 4, and various radio networks like DZMM and DWIZ.





Education

  • Ed.D. Educational Leadership & Administration, University of the East Manila
  • M.A. Teaching Social Science, Philippine Normal University
  • B.A. History, Polytechnic University of the Philippines

A Legacy of Leadership

  • Student Advocacy: Class Adviser, Club Moderator, and Prefect of Student Discipline.
  • Institutional Growth: PAASCU and FAPE-ESC Accreditation Committee Chair.
  • Faculty Representation: Former President of the UE Caloocan JHS Faculty Association (2016–2022).
  • National Influence: Resource speaker for DSWD, CEU, University of Makati, and NTC.

Major Achievements

Named a Metrobank Foundation Outstanding Filipino in 2016 and a Finalist for the ACES Award in 2024.

  • Distinguished Private School Teacher – FAPSA
  • Iconic Teacher & Asian Noble Educator Award
  • Gawad SULO for Eminent Alumni – PNU
  • Outstanding Alumnus – De La Salle Araneta University
  • Gawad Pagpapahalaga – Malabon City

Current Leadership

  • National Vice President: Metrobank Foundation NOTED.
  • Board Member: Philippine Red Cross (Caloocan) and UE-Caloocan Alumni Association.
  • Chair: International Humanitarian Law Committee.
  • Lifetime Member: Philippine Historical Association & National Historical Society.

Dr. Agoyaoy remains deeply involved in community outreach alongside his wife, Cherry May DV. Agoyaoy, focusing on nationwide educational initiatives and humanitarian service.
